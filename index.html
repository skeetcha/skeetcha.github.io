About Me

Ever since I can remember, my passion has been to create things. I remember when I was a kid that I had written a play about a cloud, the sun, the wind, and the sky (or something to that effect, it was long ago). As I've grown up, I've gained skills to give me even more outlets for my abundant creativity (because I definitely need more stuff to do!).

Nowadays, I program and run tests on graphics cards for a living, while on the side, I write my own songs and arrange music for wind ensemble, orchestra, vocal acapella, and jazz band. Recently, I've also taken to writing fiction when I'm not creating languages or worlds for people to read about.

"Creativity is an energy. It's a precious energy, and it's something to be protected. A lot of people take for granted that they're a creative person, but I know from experience, feeling it in myself, it is a magic; it is an energy. And it can't be taken for granted."
